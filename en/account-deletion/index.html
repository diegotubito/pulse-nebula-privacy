Account Deletion

Users can request account deletion directly from within the Nebula application.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Nebula app
  2. Go to Profile or Settings
  3. Select Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion request

Data removal

When an account is deleted, associated profile information and authentication data are removed from our systems, except where retention is required for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes.
